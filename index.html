Our Intelligent BIM Workflow

A systematic, technology-driven approach from reality capture to final structural models.

1. Laser Scanning

Capturing existing site conditions with millimeter accuracy using high-definition 3D laser scanners.

2. Point Cloud Data

Processing millions of data points to create a precise digital twin of the physical environment.

3. 3D Modeling (LOD 300-500)

Developing highly detailed structural models in Revit and Tekla based on point cloud references.

4. Validation & Clash Detection

Running Navisworks simulations to identify and resolve structural, architectural, and MEP clashes.

5. Final BIM & Shop Drawings

Delivering fabrication-ready 2D drawings extracted directly from the error-free 3D model.
